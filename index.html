To whoever reading this, Here is a story that reveals the truth about myself.

I used to be in the realm of Computer Science, especially software development. However, I didn’t want to be a traditional software developer focused solely on coding. I wanted to be a designer who solves users’ problems and promotes meaningful values for products. So, I transitioned to UX design to craft experiences that delight human emotions while taking care of their needs. I still value the importance of system thinking in Computer Science, which I applied to craft beautiful designs that embed cohesion, robustness, and scalability in their essence. I combine the beauty of user-centred design and the inner workings of “mechanics” to produce unique design solutions.
